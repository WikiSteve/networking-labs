# Evaluation

## Quiz

You may only write this quiz on campus and during class time.

This lab grade consists of:

- 50% from the quiz under `Evaluations > Quizzes`
- 50% from your PowerPoint submission for this lab

The quiz is intended to measure your understanding after you have completed the lab and are ready to submit. Do not take it early just to guess. Only one attempt is allowed, and the result of that first attempt is your permanent quiz grade.

The password for the quiz will be posted in the classroom and may change from class to class until the final due date.

## PowerPoint Submission

Make sure to use the submission template. Look for the PowerPoint file in the same module where you found this guide.

## Late Submissions

No late submissions are accepted. Submit early.
